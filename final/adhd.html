ADHD

What is ADHD?

ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder) is a learning disability. People with ADHD have difficulties paying attention and focusing.

  • A couple of ADHD symptoms are:

    Trouble completing tasks and or starting tasks

    Getting easily distracted by others

    Forgetting to turn in assignments

    Struggling with time management

    Interrupting others

      • What are some different types of ADHD? Predominantly inattentive ADHD Predominantly hyperactive or impulsive ADHD Combined type ADHD
